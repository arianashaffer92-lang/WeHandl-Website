Chapter One

What Are
AI Agents?

Forget the sci-fi version. Here's what an AI agent actually does for your business — in plain English.

An AI agent is software that works on your behalf — automatically. Unlike a contact form that just sends you an email, an AI agent actually does something with that lead. It responds, asks questions, qualifies the prospect, and moves them toward a booking — without you being involved at all.

⚡

It takes action

Traditional software waits for commands. AI agents interpret what's happening and respond on their own — no babysitting required.

🧠

It makes decisions

Is this lead a good fit? What should it say next? An AI agent reads context and responds intelligently, like a trained team member.

🔄

It works continuously

No shifts. No lunch breaks. No sick days. Your AI agent is handling leads at 11pm on a Saturday while you're at your kid's game.

📈

It scales with you

Whether you get 10 leads a month or 1,000, the AI agent handles every single one with the same speed and quality.

Chapter Five

How to
Get Started

You don't need to overhaul your whole business. Here's the simplest path to getting your first AI agent running — and seeing results fast.

1

Identify where leads enter your business

Website form? Google Ads? Facebook? Text messages? Map every channel where a potential customer could reach out — even the ones you're barely monitoring right now.

2

Define what a great lead looks like

What's the right budget? Service area? Timeline? Knowing this lets your AI agent pre-qualify for you — so only the right people get on your calendar.

3

Automate the first conversation

Build (or let WeHandl build) the initial response workflow — the instant reply, the qualification questions, and the booking trigger.

4

Connect your calendar

Link your scheduling tool so qualified leads can book directly — without any back-and-forth. You show up on the day. The rest handles itself.

5

Set up follow-up sequences

Build out what happens to leads who aren't ready yet. A series of 3–5 follow-up messages over 2 weeks can convert leads you would have written off.

Once this system is live, you'll respond faster, convert more leads, and free yourself up to focus on the actual work. Most WeHandl clients see it fully running within 7 days.
