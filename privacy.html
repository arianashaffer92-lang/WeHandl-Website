  • Right to Delete: You may request that we delete personal information we have collected about you, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Correct: You may request that we correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale or Sharing: We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise your California privacy rights, please contact us at info@wehandl.ai. We will respond to verified requests within 45 days.
